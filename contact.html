Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our products and services

Ordering & Payment

Placing an order with AfriMart Depot is simple! Browse our online store, select the products you'd like to purchase, and add them to your cart. When you're ready to check out, click on the cart icon, review your order, and follow the checkout process. You'll need to provide your shipping information and payment details to complete your purchase.

We accept a variety of payment methods to make your shopping experience convenient. These include Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible at info@afrimartdepot.com or +44 (0) 207 123 4567. We can usually accommodate changes if the order hasn't been processed for shipping yet. Once an order has been dispatched, it cannot be modified or cancelled.

Shipping & Delivery

We currently ship to the United Kingdom, European Union countries, the United States, Canada, and select countries in Asia and Australia. If you're unsure whether we ship to your location, please check the dropdown menu in the checkout process or contact our customer service team.

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected:

  • United Kingdom: Standard delivery (3-5 business days), Express delivery (1-2 business days)
  • European Union: 5-7 business days
  • United States & Canada: 7-10 business days
  • Rest of World: 10-14 business days

Please note that these are estimated delivery times and may vary during peak seasons or holidays.

We take special care to ensure the freshness and quality of perishable products. These items are properly packaged with insulated materials and, when necessary, shipped with cold packs to maintain optimal temperature during transit. We also expedite the shipping of perishable items to minimize transit time.

Returns & Refunds

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not satisfied with your order, you can return most unused, unopened items within 14 days of delivery for a refund or store credit. Please note that due to their nature, perishable food items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at info@afrimartdepot.com with your order number and details about the return.

To return a product, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team within 14 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number and the reason for the return.
  3. Our team will provide you with a return authorization and instructions.
  4. Package the unused, unopened items securely in their original packaging.
  5. Ship the package to the address provided in the return instructions.
  6. Once we receive and inspect the returned items, we'll process your refund or store credit.

After we receive and inspect the returned items, refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days. Once processed, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment method and financial institution.

  • Dried products (spices, herbs, dried seafood): Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, transfer to airtight containers to maintain freshness.

Shipping Information

Shipping Methods & Timeframes

We offer various shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days (UK), 5-7 business days (EU), 7-10 business days (US & Canada)
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days (UK), 2-3 business days (EU), 3-5 business days (US & Canada)

Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location, the weight of your order, and your chosen shipping method:

  • United Kingdom: Standard (£3.99), Express (£7.99)
  • European Union: Standard (£9.99), Express (£15.99)
  • United States & Canada: Standard (£12.99), Express (£19.99)
  • Rest of World: Standard (£14.99), Express (£24.99)

Orders over £50 (UK), £80 (EU), £100 (US & Canada), or £120 (Rest of World) qualify for free standard shipping.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website or by contacting our customer service team.

Customs & Import Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes imposed by the destination country. These fees are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in the purchase or shipping price. Please check your local regulations before placing an order.

Return & Refund Policy

Return Eligibility

You may return most unused, unopened items within 14 days of delivery. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unused, and in its original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Perishable goods (unless damaged or defective upon arrival)
  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable products
  • Items that have been opened or used

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team at info@afrimartdepot.com within 14 days of receiving your order.
  2. Include your order number and details about the items you wish to return.
  3. Our team will provide you with return authorization and instructions.
  4. Ship the items back to us following the provided instructions.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll send you an email to notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 3-5 business days.

Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error (you received an incorrect or defective item). If you're returning an item because of our error, we'll provide a prepaid shipping label.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive damaged or defective items, please contact us immediately at info@afrimartdepot.com. Include photos of the damaged items and packaging. We'll work with you to resolve the issue promptly, either through replacement or refund.
