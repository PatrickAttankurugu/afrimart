Product Description

Our premium red palm oil is carefully harvested and processed using traditional methods in Ghana. This vibrant oil is a staple in West African cuisine, prized for its rich flavor profile and distinctive reddish-orange color that comes from its high content of carotenoids.

Unlike industrially processed palm oils, our premium product maintains all the natural nutrients and flavor compounds that make it a treasured ingredient in traditional African cooking. The oil is sourced from small-scale, sustainable farms where palms are grown without harmful chemicals and harvested at peak ripeness.

Premium palm oil adds authentic flavor and color to traditional West African dishes

Origin & Production

Our red palm oil comes from the Western Region of Ghana, where the unique soil and climate conditions produce palm fruits with exceptional flavor characteristics. The oil is extracted using traditional methods that preserve its natural qualities and nutritional benefits.

Each batch undergoes quality testing to ensure purity and authenticity before being carefully packaged to maintain freshness during shipping.

Health Benefits

Red palm oil is known for its high content of carotenoids and tocotrienols, which are antioxidants that support overall health. It's also rich in vitamin E, which contributes to the protection of cells from oxidative stress.

Note: While palm oil is nutritious in moderation, it should be used as part of a balanced diet due to its high calorie and fat content.

Storage Information

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. After opening, refrigerate and use within 6 months for optimal freshness and quality.

Natural separation may occur. This is normal for an unrefined product and does not affect quality. Gently warm to liquefy if solidified at cooler temperatures.

How to Use

Cooking Tips

Red palm oil has a rich flavor profile and adds a distinctive taste to dishes. It's best used in traditional West African recipes where its unique flavor can enhance the overall dish. Here are some tips for cooking with our premium palm oil:

  • Ideal Cooking Temperature: Palm oil has a high smoke point, making it suitable for high-heat cooking methods like frying and sautéing.
  • Start with Small Amounts: The flavor can be quite pronounced, so begin with smaller quantities until you're familiar with its taste profile.
  • Warming: If solidified, gently warm the oil until it returns to liquid form before using.
  • Color Transfer: Be aware that the vibrant color will transfer to your dishes, which is desirable in traditional recipes.
